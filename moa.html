Mall of America

The Mall of America is the biggest mall in the United States measuring at 4.2 million square feet. It contains a ring of over 520 stores, 50 restaurants and an indoor Nickelodeon themed amusement park right in the center. The mall has 4 stories technically 5 if you include the aquarium in the basement level. An estimated 40 million people visit the mall every year! If you're hungry be sure to visit one of the several food courts that overlook the amusement park and opposing side of the mall! If you want to completely explore this major attraction in it's entirety we suggest you get busy as soon as possible! Experts estimate that it would take about 86 hours to visit every store in the Mall given you don't spend more than 10 minutes in each! If that's too much for you don't worry!
